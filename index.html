Are you eligible?

1. Do you suffer from any of the following conditions and have been diagnosed by a registered medical practitioner in Australia?

  • Sleep issues
  • Anxiety
  • Chronic Pain
  • Chronic Disease
  • Cancer Symptoms
  • Palliative Care
  • Mental Health issues
  • Migraines
  • Neurological conditions (epilepsy, seizures etc)
  • Gut Health conditions (IBS, Chron's Disease, IBD etc)

2. Have you tried conventional treatments for the condition(s) you are considering taking medical cannabis for?

3. Have the medication(s) that you have been prescribed for your condition been unsuccessful OR produced side effects that prevent you from taking your medicine(s)?

4. Are you 18 years or older?

Based on your answers, you are potentially eligible to access medicinal cannabis.

Please note that eligibility does not guarantee a prescription of Medicinal Cannabis. Eligibility means you have a higher chance of being granted approval of prescription Medicinal Cannabis as the TGA takes into consideration your current conditions and past treatments.

*TGA is the Therapeutic Goods Administration – Australia’s regulatory authority responsible for assessing applications from doctors to prescribe medical cannabis. At this time, additional approval from certain State health departments may also be required depending on your State, although it is anticipated that these State approval requirements will be merged with the TGA approval process in the future.

Based on your answers, you may not be eligible to access medicinal cannabis.

This does not mean that you will not be approved for access to medicinal cannabis with the TGA* as they will consider ALL applications from medical practitioners to access medicinal cannabis for their patients, which is where Greencare comes in.

We recommend booking an appointment with one of our doctors to discuss your health and potential treatment options. They will then go through the application process to the TGA to prescribe you Medicinal Cannabis if post consultation with you, they believe you would benefit from Medicinal Cannabis treatment.
